AILET – All India Law Entrance Test

Eligibility
Candidates must have completed 10+2 (or equivalent) examinations from a recognized board or institution. They should have secured a minimum aggregate score, which may vary from year to year.
Scope
Admission to National Law University (NLU), Delhi.
How to Apply
Online registration via the official NLU Delhi website.
Exam Structure
Three sections: English Language, Current Affairs & General Knowledge, Logical Reasoning. Total of 150 multiple-choice questions. Duration: 2 hours. Pen-and-paper-based test.
Score Pattern
Each correct answer awards 1 mark; 0.25 marks deducted for each wrong answer.
Fees
Approximately ₹3,500 for General/OBC category; ₹1,500 for SC/ST/PwD.
Exam Timing
Conducted once a year, typically in December.
Results
Announced by the last week of December.
