GATE

Name of Entrance Test
GATE
Full Form
Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering
Eligibility
Bachelor’s degree in Engineering/Science; final-year students eligible
Scope
Admission to M.Tech/PhD; PSU recruitment
How to Apply
Online via GOAPS portal https://gate.iitk.ac.in (changes per year)
Exam Structure
Online; MCQ, MSQ, NAT; sections: General Aptitude + Subject Paper
Score Pattern/Analysis
100 marks total; normalized score (for multi-session papers); rank based on raw/normalized scores
Fees
₹1800 (General); ₹900 (SC/ST/PwD/Female)
Time When Conducted
February
Number of Times/Year
1
Time for Results
Mid-March (~1 month after exam)
