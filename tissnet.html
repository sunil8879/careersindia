TISSNET – Tata Institute of Social Sciences National Entrance Test

Eligibility
Bachelor’s degree in any discipline. Final-year students can apply.
Scope
Admission to MA programs at TISS campuses.
How to Apply
Online registration via the official TISS website.
Exam Structure
Computer-based test. Sections: English Proficiency, Maths & Logical Reasoning, General Awareness.
Score Pattern
Scores used for shortlisting for further rounds (PIT/PI).
Fees
Varies, approx ₹1000-₹2000.
Exam Timing
Typically in January/February.
Results
Announced a few weeks after the exam.
Note: As of 2025, TISSNET has been discontinued. Admissions are now based on CAT or CUET PG scores. The details above reflect its previous format.
