Figure Series

What is a Figure Series?

A Figure Series is a sequence of diagrams that follow a specific pattern or rule. Your task is to analyze the sequence, identify the underlying logic, and determine which of the answer figures would come next in the series.

How to Solve Figure Series Questions

Look for consistent changes between each figure in the series. The pattern can be based on one or more of the following rules:

  • Rotation: Elements rotate clockwise or counter-clockwise by a fixed angle (e.g., 45°, 90°).
  • Movement/Translation: Elements move to a new position (e.g., corner to corner, up, down).
  • Addition/Deletion: New elements are added or existing elements are removed in each step.
  • Transformation: An element changes its shape, size, or form.
  • Shading/Filling: Elements alternate between being filled (solid) and unfilled (outline).
  • Combination: Two or more of the above rules are applied simultaneously.
