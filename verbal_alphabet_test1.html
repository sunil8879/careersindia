ALPHABET TEST

Theory: Introduction

Some Important points to Remember:

  1. Vowels – The letters A, E, I, O and U are called vowels.
  2. Consonants – The letters other than vowels are called consonants.
  3. Letters A to M are called the first half of English alphabet.
  4. Letters N to Z are called the second half of English alphabet.
  5. Left to right of English alphabet is from A to Z.
  6. Right to left of English alphabet is from Z to A.
  7. nth letter to the left of mth letter from right end = (m+n)th letter from the right end.
  8. nth letter to the right of mth letter from left end = (m+n)th letter from the left end.
  9. nth letter to the left of mth letter from left end = (m-n)th letter from the left end.
  10. nth letter to the right of mth letter from right end = (m-n)th letter from the right end.

TYPE 1: ALPHABETICAL ORDER OF WORDS

Alphabetical arrangement means arranging the words in the sequence in which they occur in the dictionary.

Page 124 of 309

Sometimes, two or more words begin with the same letter, in such cases look for the alphabetical arrangement of the second letter. If the second letter is also the same, look for the alphabetical arrangement of the third letter and so on.

Question: Example 1

If the following five words are arranged in alphabetical order, which word will come in the middle?

  1. Sing
  2. Read
  3. Dance
  4. Write
  5. Play
Explanation

These words begin with the letters 'S', 'R', 'D', 'W' and 'P' respectively. Their order in English alphabet is 'D' (Dance), 'P' (Play), 'R' (Read), 'S' (Sing), 'W' (Write). Hence, the word that comes in the middle (third position) will begin with 'R'.

So, the correct answer is (b) Read.

Question: Example 1

If first half of the English alphabet is written in reverse order, then find the 15th letter from the right. (Canara Bank 2008)

  1. A
  2. B
  3. C
  4. D
  5. E
Diagram: Solution
M --- C B A N O --- Z
Reversed 13 letters of 1st half
Unchanged 13 letters of 2nd half

The 15th letter from the right is the 2nd letter of the reversed first half. Counting from Z, the first 13 are unchanged (N-Z). The 14th is A, the 15th is B.

Hence, the correct answer is (b) B.
