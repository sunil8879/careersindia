ICAR AIEEA (UG/PG)

Full Form
Indian Council of Agricultural Research All India Entrance Examination for Admission
Eligibility
UG: 12th with PCM/PCB/PCMB; PG: Bachelor's in Agriculture or allied subjects
Scope
Admission to UG/PG programs in Agriculture & Allied Sciences in ICAR institutes and agri universities
How to Apply
Apply at icar.nta.nic.in
Exam Structure
UG: 150 MCQs (PCM/PCB), PG: 160 MCQs based on discipline – 2.5 hours
Score Pattern & Analysis
+4 for correct, -1 for wrong. Percentile and merit list used.
Fees
UG: ₹800 (Gen), ₹400 (SC/ST); PG: ₹1100 (Gen), ₹550 (SC/ST)
Time Conducted
June–July
Frequency
Once a year
Result Time
Results in 4–5 weeks
