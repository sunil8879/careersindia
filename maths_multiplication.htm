MULTIPLICATION OF NUMBERS

 

1.      Numbers close to 100

101 is 1 greater than 100. So, write 1 alongside 101.

111 is 11 greater than 100. So, write 11 alongside 111.

Multiply 1 with 11. Answer is 11.-------------A

Now, 11 + 1 = 12. Add 12 to 100. Answer is 112----B

So, write 11200-------C

Now, add A to C.

Answer is 11200 + 11 = 11211

 

2.      94 and 98

94 is 6 less than 100.write -6 alongside 94

98 is 2 less than 100. Write -2 alongside 98

Now multiply -6 with -2. Answer is 12A

Now, -6-2 = -8

Add -8 to 100. Answer is 92------------B

Now write 9200----------------C

Add A to C

Answer is 9200 + 12 = 9212

 

3.      109 and 94

109 is 9 more than 100. Write 9 along side 109

94 is 6 less than 100. So, write -6 along side 94

Multiply -6 with 9 = -54--------------------A

Now, -6+9 = 3

Add 3 to 100. Answer is 103---------------------B

 

Now, write 10300-----------------C

Add A to C

Answer is 10300-54 = 10246

2.      46x26

We can write this as :

46

26

Multiply 6x6 = 36 and write it on the extreme right

Multiply 4x2 = 8 and write it on the extreme left

Then, (6x4) + (6x2) = 24 + 12 = 36. Write it in the middle.

So, it becomes

8|36|36

Consider the last 36. Write 6 and carry 3. So,it would be 6 on the extreme right--------------A

Consider the middle 36. We r carrying 3. So, it becomes 36 + 3 = 39. Write 9 and carry 3. So, it would be 9---------------B

Consider 8. We r carrying 3. So, it would be 8 + 3 = 11----------------------------C

From A,B and C, we get :
