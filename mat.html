MAT – Management Aptitude Test

Eligibility
Bachelor’s degree in any discipline. Final-year students can apply.
Scope
Accepted by over 600 B-schools across India.
How to Apply
Online registration via the AIMA website.
Exam Structure
Five sections: Language Comprehension, Mathematical Skills, Data Analysis & Sufficiency, Intelligence & Critical Reasoning, Indian & Global Environment. Duration: 150 minutes. Available in Paper-Based Test (PBT), Computer-Based Test (CBT), and Internet-Based Test (IBT) modes.
Score Pattern
Composite score out of 800; separate score for Indian & Global Environment.
Fees
Approximately ₹1,950 for one mode; ₹3,100 for two modes.
Exam Timing
Conducted four times a year – February, May, September, and December.
Results
Announced within a few weeks after the exam.
