About Accreditations

  • NCVT: National Council for Vocational Training

    Sets the national standards for ITI courses and trade skills (e.g., Fitter, Electrician). A national-level certification.

  • SCVT: State Council for Vocational Training

    Operates at the state level, similar to NCVT. Some trades may only have SCVT approval within that state.

  • QCI: Quality Council of India

    An autonomous body that assesses and accredits the overall quality standards of an institution (like NAAC for colleges or NBA for technical courses).

  • NSQF: National Skills Qualifications Framework

    A national framework that organizes qualifications into levels (from 1 to 10) based on knowledge and skill. It helps in comparing different vocational courses.

  • NAPS/NATS: Apprenticeship Schemes

    Indicates the institute is registered for national apprenticeship programs (NAPS/NATS), allowing students to get on-the-job training with companies.
